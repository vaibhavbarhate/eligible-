The flaw of choice overload

We are inundated with too many choices too often. While this is marketed as a benefit of online dating, we must realise that a larger set of matches doesn’t necessarily translate to more genuine connections.

The flaw of choice overload

Your motivation for using a dating app is to find romance, and often a long-term relationship. Dating apps, on the other hand, are motivated by things like increasing their number of users.

This in turn leads to users having far less chance at 'matching' with like-minded others who share common interests, and swiping left becomes a necessary habit.

Our members (‘ELI’S’)

Eligible believes that like-minded people naturally gravitate towards each other. This can happen because individuals with similar backgrounds, interests, and values are more likely to enjoy each other's company and find common ground. Shared experiences and perspectives can create a sense of connection and understanding among like-minded individuals.

Whilst it's important to note that diversity of thought and perspective can also be valuable as it can lead to new ideas and perspectives, we believe that sharing key values and coming from similar backgrounds are critical for great matches.

We are not saying other values and backgrounds are wrong. We are simply saying that, in order to maximize our success of bringing together people who will have a greater propensity to 'click', we have some values we want our users to share. 

If you ‘fit’ these values, then Eligible is for you.

SELF-CONFIDENCE

In order to be successful in anything you do, you must first believe that you are capable. Having this self-confidence allows you to go after what you want and see your efforts through without giving up.

DISCIPLINE

Along with drive and patience, successful people also have discipline. Once they are motivated to accomplish something, they work hard to achieve it and are consistent in their efforts. Having a strong work ethic makes people more successful.

OPTIMISIM

Successful people are typically optimists. This doesn't mean that you can't and shouldn't be realistic, but successful people are usually able to stay positive despite challenges. 

COMMITMENT

Research indicates that both levels of commitment and perceived mutuality of commitment account for relationship prosperity. Commitment is one of the examples of relationship values that is a major contributor to a couple’s well-being. 

ASPIRATION

To be successful at anything, you first need an idea of how you want to succeed. Successful people are known for having clear-cut goals that they work hard to reach.
